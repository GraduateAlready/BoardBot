About

Board climbing strengthens grip and finger strength, all while pushing climbers to develop their technique. It strengthens the lumbar, lats, and biceps, while working the smaller stabilizing muscles. Standard boards make training easy because the problems have already been set. Spray walls take a bit more consideration but can offer even greater advantages than standard boards.

If you climb primarily on Moon, Tension, and Kilter boards for your board training, you will notice that you become used to the distances between holds. Certain moves between boards begin to feel identical because, in many ways, they are. A spray wall keeps you on your toes and trying different sorts of movements. Another problem with standard boards is that people love setting with a kicker. A spray wall allows complete autonomy and you can produce movement that truly cannot be replicated by many of the more famous board problems.
